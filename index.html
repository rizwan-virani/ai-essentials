How to use this platform

  1. Read the Assessment Mechanics & Career Guidance cards first. Understand the landscape: how the AI Essentials CompCert assessment works, scoring, question styles, and where practical AI fluency fits in your career.
  2. Deep-dive the five Domain Study cards. Each opens a rigorous, scenario-driven reading interface that mirrors a full prep manual.
  3. Lock in the terms with Rapid Recall Flashcards. 100 cards per domain plus a Master Acronym Drill, powered by spaced repetition — grade each card honestly and the scheduler resurfaces what you're forgetting and retires what you know.
  4. Validate your knowledge with the Domain Quizzes. Focused questions per domain, drawn at random and graded with full rationale for every option.
  5. Train practical skills in the PBQ Simulators. 30 interactive performance-based decision tasks across prompt construction, output evaluation, conversation & iteration, responsible use, and automation, plus a drag-and-drop taxonomy mapping engine that has you sort real-world scenarios into the correct categories.
  6. Benchmark readiness with the full-length, timed Mock Exam. 75 questions weighted across the five study domains, with a countdown timer and a scoring dashboard that reports percentage mastery.
  7. Get hands-on in the Labs. Twenty interactive practice labs spanning every study domain — flip each card to review its objectives, then launch a full-screen console to build prompts, evaluate outputs, and make responsible-use decisions.
  8. Plan your path in the Supplemental Learning Hub. Open the Industry Certification Explorer to compare 42 industry certifications across 12 vendors, with costs, exam blueprints, and salary and role insights.

Notice: This platform is optimized for desktop or laptop computers running the Google Chrome browser. Interactive elements, including the hands-on labs, terminal simulations, and drag-and-drop activities, require a mouse and keyboard to function correctly. These features may not display or operate properly on mobile devices.

What this is

  • An academic preparation resource that tests your knowledge dynamically with original, randomized questions and hands-on simulators.
  • A comprehensive study portal covering every objective in the official AI Essentials course outline.
  • Free, open-source, and self-paced. Everything saves to your browser — come back where you left off.

  • Not a data-science or machine-learning-engineering course, and not a guarantee of a passing score. It builds practical, end-user AI fluency — judgment, not memorization.
